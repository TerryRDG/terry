    Part 3:

    It can be observed in the Bank statement that there are some prenumbered checks missing in the statement. The three possible reasons for the same could be:

    Checks issued by the company, but not yet presented by them – Company records the payment when check is issued. However, it appears in the statement when the same is presented in the Bank. The customers can present it within the deadline. Hence, the order differs based on when the customer presents the checks.

    Checks of previous months cleared in the present month – Checks issued in the previous months will be recorded in company’s books in the previous month. However, they appear in Bank statement when they are presented. They might be presented in the later months, which might result in missing of checks in order.

    Cancelled checks – Sometimes, checks may be cancelled after issuance by the company, due to incorrect amounts, names, etc. In such cases, the checks are not even presented to the bank. Those check numbers will not even be present, resulting in missing of checks in order.
